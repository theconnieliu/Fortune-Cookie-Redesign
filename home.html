Directions

For Driving:

From the East Bay

  • Take the Bay Bridge (I-80 West toward San Francisco)
  • Use the right 2 lanes to take exit 2C to merge onto Fremont St
  • Merge onto Fremont Street
  • Use the left 2 lanes to turn left onto Pine St
  • Turn right onto Powell St
  • Turn right onto Jackson St
  • Turn right onto Ross Alley

From the South Bay

  • Take US-101 N toward San Francisco
  • Keep right at the fork to continue on I-80 E, follow signs for Bay Bridge / Interstate 80
  • Use the right 2 lanes to take Exit 2 for Fourth Street
  • Use the left 2 lanes to turn left onto Bryant St
  • Use the left 2 lanes to turn left onto 3rd St
  • Continue onto Kearny St
  • Use the left 2 lanes to turn left after Chipotle Mexican Grill
  • Turn right after Starbucks
  • Continue onto Stockton St Tunnel
  • Turn right onto Jackson St
  • Turn right onto Ross Alley

From the North Bay

  • Take the Golden Gate Bridge (US-101 South)
  • Continue to follow Presidio Parkway
  • Continue onto Richardson Ave
  • Continue onto Lombard St
  • Use the right 2 lanes to turn right after Comfort Inn By the Bay
  • Use the left 2 lanes to turn left onto Broadway
  • Continue onto Robert C Levy tunnel
  • Continue onto Broadway
  • Turn right onto Stockton
  • Turn left after Western Union
  • Turn right onto Ross Alley

Cable Car Schedule

For Public Transportation:

From the Powell Street Cable Car (Powell Street & Market - Bay + Taylor line)

  • Exit Powell St & Jackson St
  • Walk right down Jackson St past Stockton. Ross Alley will be on the right-hand side, between Stockton and Kearny.

From the Ferry Building

  • Walk to Clay St & Drumm St
  • Take the 1 Muni (Geary + 33rd Avenue)
  • Exit Sacramento St & Grant Ave
  • Walk right up Grant Street. Make a left on Washington, and Ross Alley will be immediately on your right-hand side.

From Fisherman's Wharf

  • Walk to Columbus Ave & Bay St
  • Take the 30 Muni (Market + Fourth Street)
  • Exit Stockton St & Jackson St
  • Walk left on Jackson, Ross Alley will be on the right-hand side.
